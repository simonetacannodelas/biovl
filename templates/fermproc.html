Bioprocesses

Introduction

The world is evolving and we need to be prepare to help into a more sustainable future. To do so, DTU and Auckland University are developing a free platform with the aim to encourage bioengineering students to learn more about bioprocess models and being more prepare to implemented them in their society.

FermProc is the results of this efforts. In FermProc, you can do:

Optimize the production of the microorganism of your choice

Control the production of your very unstable bioprocess

Learn how to code your own fermentation model in Python
