Mechanistic models

Mathematical mechanistic models are like puzzles. You can choose how many pieces you want to put and the more pieces, the most complex it becomes.

A model aims to represent an image of what it is happening in the process and are involved in the understanding, the prediction and control, and the reproducibility.

Now we will focus in bioconversion models and some of their most important parameters.

Yields

Why is yield so important?. Yields indicate a maximum of the amount of compound that the microorganisms can produce per reactant

It will play an important factor in the economical decision if a process will be economically feasible or not.
